Dumpling:

Description

Dumpling is a traditional food in my home country Mongolia. Although it is originated from China , every Mongolian citizen has a fond for eating Dumpling. Especially during "Tsagaan sar" (which means Lunar new year in Mongolia) dumpling is the most consumed and delighted dish.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Salt
    •

    Steps

    1. Gradually add water to flour and mix into smooth dough
    2. Cut the dough into small pieces. Piece of dough for each wrapping should weigh about 12-15g
    3. Roll though dough out into a 9cm circle using floured rolling pin
    4. Mix ground beef, -onion, salt and pepper to make the filling
    5. Put filling into the wrapping and pinch wrappers edge together
    6. Steam the dumpling for 20-25 minutes
